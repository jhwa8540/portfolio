Reflections

We followed a linear and logical process of Researching a problem, presenting a ideas to solve the issue, iterate from user testing with a range of prototypes (low~high fidelty), and finishing with an end product that encooperated all the feedbacks recieved.

However, due to circumstances our user testing could not reach the validity that we intended. We encountered a major problem when it came to user testing as we had to ask people to pretend they were drunk/place them in a scenario where they were intoxicated and use our interface. Hence the one thing I would have changed for a more valid iterative process would have been give participants alcohol (which pretty sure isn't allowed) or conduct the user testing near a bar with potentially more intoxicated people.
